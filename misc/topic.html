Chosen Technology/Topic

Our chosen topic is the educational uses of ChatGPT, a widely recognized AI toll that has over 1.6 billion users worldwide.

As ChatGPT continues to gain popularity in 2023, it is essential to explore its role in education, particularly its impact on students seeking help with homework, assignments, and quizzes. This raises importnt questions about how to regulate and control the uses of ChatGPT to ensure that it is not being misused for academic dishonesty or other unethical purposes.
